# Statement of Work
## Meridian Financial Group — Customer Onboarding Portal Modernisation
### Version 1.2 | Execution Copy | February 28, 2026

---

## 1. Project Overview

This Statement of Work governs the delivery of a modernised customer onboarding portal for Meridian Financial Group. The engagement covers discovery, design, development, testing, and deployment of a web-based onboarding experience integrated with existing Meridian infrastructure.

**Engagement Start Date:** March 10, 2026
**Planned Completion Date:** June 13, 2026 (14 weeks)
**Contract Value:** $680,000 USD fixed fee

---

## 2. Scope of Work

### 2.1 In Scope

- Design and development of a responsive web-based customer onboarding portal
- Document upload capability with client-side validation
- KYC-compatible architecture to be designed collaboratively in Sprint 2, subject to API availability confirmed by client
- Integration with Meridian identity management system (SSO)
- Core banking data read capability via available Finacle APIs, subject to Meridian IT change management approval and API documentation availability
- Basic reporting dashboard for onboarding funnel metrics
- User acceptance testing support (2 weeks, client-led)

### 2.2 Out of Scope

- Mobile native application development
- Changes to core Finacle banking system configuration
- Third-party KYC provider contract negotiation or procurement
- Ongoing post-go-live support (covered under separate maintenance agreement)
- Data migration from legacy onboarding system

### 2.3 Assumptions

- Client will provide KYC API credentials and documentation no later than end of Week 3
- Finacle API documentation and sandbox access will be available by Week 4, subject to Meridian IT change management process
- Client product owner will be available for a minimum of 3 hours per week for story review, sprint planning, and UAT sign-off
- Initial backlog will be developed collaboratively with the client product owner during Weeks 1-2
- UAT will be conducted and signed off by client within the 2-week UAT window

## 4. Delivery Methodology

The engagement will follow a Scrum-based agile delivery approach with 2-week sprints.

### 4.1 Backlog and Sprint Planning

The initial product backlog will be developed collaboratively between the delivery team and the client product owner during Weeks 1-2. Sprint planning sessions will occur at the start of each sprint. The client product owner is responsible for story prioritisation and acceptance.

### 4.2 Milestones

| Milestone | Target Week |
|---|---|
| Kickoff and project setup | Week 1 |
| Initial backlog draft available for client review | Week 2 |
| Sprint 1 complete — foundational portal scaffolding | Week 4 |
| Sprint 2 complete — document upload, basic auth | Week 6 |
| Sprint 3 complete — KYC integration (subject to API availability) | Week 8 |
| Sprint 4 complete — Finacle read integration (subject to change approval) | Week 10 |
| Sprint 5 complete — reporting, polish, performance | Week 12 |
| UAT period | Weeks 13-14 |
| Go-live | End of Week 14 |

### 4.3 Runbook and Operational Documentation

Operational documentation and runbook will be delivered as part of the go-live package at the end of Week 14.

---

## 5. Governance

### 5.1 Status Reporting

Weekly written status reports will be provided by the delivery lead every Friday. Reports will cover: sprint progress, risks, issues, and upcoming sprint goals.

### 5.2 Escalation

Issues unable to be resolved at delivery level will be escalated to the PMO Sponsor within 2 business days.

---

## 6. Acceptance and Sign-Off

Project sign-off requires written acceptance from the Meridian PMO Sponsor confirming completion of all in-scope deliverables and UAT sign-off. Partial acceptance is not permitted under this agreement.
